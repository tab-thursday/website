Vacation from Distraction

The internet is full of distractions - from social media alerts and top ten articles to cat videos and dog memes. It's far too easy to get sucked down the rabbit hole of procrastination, and the millions of tabs you likely have open in your browser right now sure aren't helping.

The solution? Turn those tumultuous tabs into organized to-do lists!

How It Works

Top scientists at prestigious universities have proven that laser-focused attention on a single task increases productivity and overall happiness tenfold. Under guidance from these principles, we've created a web application that's guaranteed to help you live a better, more organized lifestyle. Just follow these three simple steps.

Create

Sign up for an account by providing us with some basic information.

Organize

Convert all your tabs into organized and more managable to-do lists.

Relax

Live a more productive life free from clutter and the mania of multitasking.
